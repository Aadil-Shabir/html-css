HOUSE RULES

In an effort to maintain a calm and peaceful environment for all out clients we kindly request that all mobile phones are switched off or silenced during your visit to our salon.


Gift vouchers must be redeemed within three months of the date of issue, failure to do so will render them invalid.


If in the event that you are not satisfied with the service produced by david rae salons we will be more than happy to corrent within 7 days of the appointment and the hair must be untreated by any other professional apart from David Rae Salons staff to guarantee the work has been carried out by david rae salons and no other chemicals or cutting has been applied

Out of consideration for other clients, clients who are more than 20 minutes late for an appointment will have their appointment shortened or may have to reschedule.


Promotional offers can not be used with any other offers currently running.


Anyone who fails to give 24hrs cancellation notice will be charged 50% of the service cost or will lose their deposit.
